Open Road Registry

Verified club identity index for Second Life motorcycle clubs.

Purpose

Open Road Registry exists for one reason: clarity. It helps clubs confirm who is official, who to contact, and whether a club identity is verified without politics, rankings, or opinion.

What the Registry Does

  • Creates an official club profile page (visibility is the club’s choice)
  • Verifies identity through a simple challenge process
  • Displays status clearly: verified pending inactive suspended
  • Allows clubs to list public contacts (optional)
  • Shows a last activity heartbeat (so profiles don’t become stale)

What the Registry Does Not Do

  • No leaderboards
  • No “best club” scoring
  • No reputation ranking
  • No alliance control
  • No public drama systems

This is intentional. The Registry is infrastructure — not a governing body.

Verification

Verification confirms that the registering President has control of the club entry. The Registry uses a challenge code approach. When verified, the profile displays the date of verification.

Verification is not an endorsement. It simply means the club identity is confirmed.

Status Definitions

  • Verified Identity confirmed and active.
  • Pending Registered but not yet verified.
  • Inactive Verified previously, but no recent heartbeat updates.
  • Suspended Temporarily hidden due to confirmed impersonation or abuse.

Neutrality Policy

Open Road Registry does not participate in MC politics, alliances, rivalries, or disputes. The Registry maintains only identity integrity:

  • Prevents impersonation and obvious fraud
  • Prevents duplicate name conflicts where possible
  • Maintains uptime and data integrity

How to Join

Clubs register and manage their profile through the admin page: /admin.html

  • Register your club
  • Save your private token (keep it secret)
  • Request a challenge code
  • Confirm verification
  • Set public contacts (optional)

If you keep your profile private, it will not appear in public search.
