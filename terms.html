Open Road Registry

Terms of Use and Neutrality Policy

1. Purpose

Open Road Registry exists solely to provide identity verification and public listing services for Second Life motorcycle clubs.

The Registry does not govern, rank, endorse, or evaluate clubs.

2. Verification Is Not Endorsement

A Verified status indicates that the registering President has successfully completed the identity confirmation process.

Verification does not imply:

  • Approval of behavior
  • Support of alliances
  • Judgment of quality
  • Endorsement of reputation

3. Neutrality

Open Road Registry remains neutral in all matters of:

  • MC politics
  • Alliances and rivalries
  • Inter club disputes
  • Leadership conflicts

The Registry will not mediate disagreements or take sides.

4. Suspension and Removal

A club profile may be suspended only for the following reasons:

  • Confirmed impersonation or fraudulent registration
  • Duplicate name conflicts that cannot be resolved
  • Technical abuse of the system

Suspension is administrative and does not reflect on the character or culture of a club.

5. Inactive Status

If a club fails to send periodic activity confirmation, its status may be marked as Inactive automatically.

Inactive status is informational only and not disciplinary.

6. Data Accuracy

Clubs are responsible for maintaining accurate profile information.

The Registry provides tools to update club details but does not guarantee accuracy of submitted content.

7. Limitation of Liability

Open Road Registry is an informational identity system. It is not responsible for actions, disputes, or activities of registered clubs.

8. Policy Updates

These terms may be updated to maintain clarity and operational stability.
